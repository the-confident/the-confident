§03 Our model

Build once. Share with everyone.

Most agencies build something for your council and then move on.

We build something for your council — and then give it to every other council, for free.

This is the Build to Share model, and it changes the economics of council digital services entirely. One council funds the development. The feature is released open-source on Drupal.org. Every council in the UK, Ireland, and beyond can use it at no cost, forever.

Your council gets a solution built precisely to your requirements. You get the credit for funding shared public digital infrastructure. And you benefit from every improvement the community makes to it after release.

"Public funds become public code. That's how it should work."

Aligns with MHCLG's Local Digital Declaration and the Irish Government's Build to Share initiative.

§04 How we work

Small by choice.
Not by accident.

When you contact us, the person who replies is the person doing the work.

We deliberately keep The Confident small. Fewer clients means every client gets our full attention — not a junior account manager and a ticket queue.

When something goes wrong, we already know about it. When you need a sensible answer on a Friday afternoon, you get one.

We are selective about what we take on. If a project isn't one we can genuinely excel at, we'll tell you — and point you in the direction of someone who can. We'd rather turn down work than do it badly.
